Call For Papers

Tracks of Conference:Original research articles in the areas of Electrical, Electronics and Computer Science streams are solicited. Topics include but are not limited to:

  • Intelligent Control of drives
  • Smart grid
  • Grid integration of renewable resources
  • Advanced Power electronics
  • Intelligent measurement
  • Soft Computing applications
  • Antenna and Propagation
  • Advanced Communication Techniques
  • Image/Speech/Audio Processing
  • Microelectronics
  • VLSI/Embedded Systems
  • Digital Signal Processing
  • Security in Communication
  • Natural Language Processing
  • Computer Networks
  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Machine Learning
  • Computational Biology
  • Database Management
  • Data Mining
  • Cloud Computing
  • Distributed/ Parallel Processing systems
  • Algorithm Analysis

Paper Submission

Length of the paper should be limited to a maximum of 6 pages in IEEE double column format. All papers will be peer reviewed and the review comments will be communicated to the authors. At least one author should register for the conference. All selected papers will be included in the conference proceedings and IEEE Explore. Soft copy of the same shall be made available to the participants.
